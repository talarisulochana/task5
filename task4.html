Men clothing

    Men's fashion is often characterized by more classic and timeless designs, with an emphasis on simplicity and functionality. In addition to clothing, 
    menswear also includes accessories like belts, ties, hats, and shoes, all designed to complement and enhance the overall look of an outfit.




    
 womens clothing

    Women's clothing in India nowadays consists of both formal and casual wear such as gowns, pants, shirts, and tops. 
    Traditional Indian clothing such as the kurti have been combined with jeans to form part of casual attire.




    
kids wear

    Children's clothing or kids' clothing is clothing for children who have not yet grown to full height. 
    Children's clothing is often more casual than adult clothing, fit for play and rest.
